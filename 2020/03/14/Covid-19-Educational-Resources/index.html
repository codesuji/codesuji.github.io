COVID-19 Educational Resources

Read Time: 2 minutes

In these crazy times parents are looking for, among other things, good ways to occupy their childrens’ time in a wholesome fashion. I didn’t want my childrens’ learning to just stop. To move the ball forward, at least a little, I have currated a list of education related resources to help in that process. My goal was to find things that are in the spirit of fun and learning. Nothing here is officially endorsed by me. I only provide these links because our family has found them helpful, I hope you do to. Stay safe, stay sane.

Biology

Zoo America - Zoo America features an animal (11:00AM daily)

Cincinnati Zoo - Cincinnati Zoo features an animal (3:00PM daily)

San Diego Zoo Live cams - Animal cams

Snapshot Safari - Crowdsourced projects to classify animals from nature cameras

Virtual museum tours

Metropolitan Opera - Live performance every night from their catalog of recorded events.

Seattle Symphony Orchestra - Free live stream performances

Geography

Location Estimater - Guess the world location from Google-van images

GeoGuesser - Guess the world location from Google-van images (nice, but kind of naggy)

Language

Duolingo - Learn or practice another language

Hello Chinese - Learn Chinese
